# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

AU/global recruiters and hiring managers evaluating frontend-focused fullstack engineers. They land here from a CV link, LinkedIn, or referral. Time-pressed. Want fast proof of role fit: shipped work, stack match, seniority signal, ease of contact. Secondary: founders/clients considering contract work; dev peers checking craft.

## Product Purpose

Personal portfolio for Renz Ivan Enguio. Convert qualified visitors into interviews. Demonstrate eight years of shipped frontend/cross-platform work (Vue, React, TypeScript, Quasar+Capacitor), AI-augmented engineering practice (Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, MCP, custom agents), and backend range (GCP). Make the CV download, project links, and contact frictionless. Design itself is a portfolio piece — the site must read as crafted, not templated.

## Brand Personality

Confident, technical, calm. Engineer who ships and reads design. Not enthusiastic-bootcamp, not corporate-stiff, not AI-bro. Three words: precise, current, grounded. Voice writes the way a senior eng writes a PR description — direct, evidence-led, no filler.

## Anti-references

- AI-slop SaaS landing pages: purple-to-cyan hero gradient, glass cards, hero-metric template, "AI-powered" buzzword wash.
- Corporate / stiff agency sites: stock photography, generic "we deliver excellence" tone, uncommitted neutral palette.
- Overly busy maximalist sites: particle fields, parallax everywhere, effects competing for attention.
- Generic dev-portfolio template: gradient hero + identical project cards + skills grid + obligatory dark mode toggle.

## Design Principles

1. **Design is the demo.** The site itself proves the craft claim. If the portfolio looks templated, the rest is noise.
2. **Recruiter-readable first, peer-readable second.** Stack, seniority, shipped work scannable in 10 seconds. Personality earned in the next 60.
3. **Commit to a register per variant.** Restrained + tinted neutrals, or one committed color, or full editorial palette — no waffling middle.
4. **Evidence over adjectives.** Real product links (etel.com.au apps, Coffee Mobile, Proxima, Spondise, Dotactics) carry more weight than "passionate about clean code."
5. **AI-augmented is the edge, not the costume.** Surface the practice (skills, agents, MCP, orchestration) without performing it. No glowing AI badges.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.2 AA contrast minimum on all text and interactive states.
- Full keyboard navigation, visible focus rings, skip-to-content.
- `prefers-reduced-motion` respected — disable animation, not just slow it.
- Semantic landmarks, real heading order, alt text on every project image.
- Body text ≥16px, line length capped 65–75ch.
